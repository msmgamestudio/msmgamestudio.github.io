Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

MSM Game Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Aqua Accord: Water Sort Puzzle mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Account Information: If you choose to use the Global Leaderboard and save your progress online, we offer authentication via Google Sign-In. Through this, we collect your Name, Email Address, and a unique User ID.
  • Gameplay & Approximate Location: To facilitate country-wise and global rankings, we collect your in-game progress (levels cleared, coins) and your approximate location (country code only).
  • Usage & Device Data: We automatically collect diagnostics, crash logs, app interactions, and Device/Advertising IDs to ensure app stability and serve personalized content.

Note: You can play the game as a Guest without signing in, in which case Account Information and Approximate Location are not collected.

2. Data Retention and Deletion

How to Delete Your Data: You have full control over your personal information. If you wish to permanently delete your account, leaderboard rankings, and associated data, you can do so directly within the app.

Navigate to: Settings > Delete Account Data.

Alternatively, you may request the deletion of your data by emailing us at contact.msmgames@gmail.com.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to facilitate our app's core functionality, analytics, and advertising. These services have their own Privacy Policies regarding how they handle your data:

  • Google Play Services & Billing: Used to process secure in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not process or store your payment details.
  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics): Used for secure login, hosting leaderboards, tracking app crashes, and analyzing player interactions to improve gameplay.
  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements. AdMob uses Advertising IDs and tracking technologies to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Local Notifications

Our app uses local push notifications to remind you of in-game events. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data through these notifications. All scheduling is handled locally on your device, and you can disable them anytime from the in-game Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take necessary actions to remove that information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, or if you need assistance with data management, please do not hesitate to contact us at contact.msmgames@gmail.com.
